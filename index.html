Lebanon


What's Going on


Lebanon needs your help.

The Beirut port blast on 4 August ripped a city to shreds and reopened old wounds for a fragile population already facing civil unrest, an ongoing socio-economic crisis, and the COVID-19 pandemic.

The people of Lebanon are more vulnerable than ever before, with more than 1 million people living below the poverty line and 1.5 million refugees.


How to Help


These NGOs provide healthcare, shelter, food and medical aid to those affected by the disaster. They have a track record of being on the ground, reliable, and volunteer-based. Alternatively, these GoFundMe pages are unique projects started by people from diverse backgrounds with a mission to help.

Lebanese Red Cross

On-ground volunteering and medical emergency services

Offre Joie

Volunteer movement to rebuild homes and clean up debris

Beit El Baraka

Comprehensive support for families: homes, food, and medical
